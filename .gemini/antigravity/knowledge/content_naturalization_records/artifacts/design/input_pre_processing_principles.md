This pattern addresses the gap between messiness (low effort) and high-quality AI output (high precision).

### Step 1: Noise Removal (Non-invasive)

Remove redundant fillers, spelling errors, and logical circularity without changing the core argument.

### Step 2: Intent Calibration (Intensity Spectrum)

Determine the "Pre-processing Intensity":

- **Low**: Just fix typos (Light).
- **High**: Reconstruct the logic based on context (Rich).

### Step 3: Priming (Dialectic Output)

Instead of just "better text", produce a "primed input" that is optimized for specific theorems (e.g., geared for `/noe` or `/ene`).
