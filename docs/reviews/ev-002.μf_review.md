## 修正提案

```python
    # PURPOSE: H-series (感情/動機) の推薦テスト
    def test_recommend_h_series(self, advisor: AttractorAdvisor):
        """H-series (感情/動機) の推薦テスト"""
        rec = advisor.recommend("How do you feel about this? I am anxious.")
        assert "H" in rec.series
        assert rec.oscillation in (OscillationType.CLEAR, OscillationType.POSITIVE)

    # PURPOSE: K-series (タイミング/文脈) の推薦テスト
    def test_recommend_k_series(self, advisor: AttractorAdvisor):
        """K-series (タイミング/文脈) の推薦テスト"""
        rec = advisor.recommend("Is now the right time? Check the schedule.")
        assert "K" in rec.series
        assert len(rec.workflows) > 0
```
