# Hegemonikón: Workflow Catalog (v4.0)

## 1. Overview

Hegemonikón utilizes a hierarchical hub-and-spoke model for workflow management, enabling 100% information density while adhering to platform character limits.

## 2. Standard Operational Workflows

### 2.1. Initialization & Closure

- **`/boot` (Session Initialization)**: 18-step hierarchical sequence (Push-only context). Phases include Identity Loading, Change Tracking, State Audit, and Knowledge Ingestion.
- **`/bye` (Session Closure)**: Ritual for context crystallization and Handoff generation in SBAR/ADR format.

### 2.2. Intelligence & Research

- **`/sop` (Sophia Research)**: Standardized Perplexity workflow with `surf`, `deep`, `prag`, and `track` modes.
- **`/eat` (Content Digestion)**: Naturalizing external knowledge into Hegemonikón ontology via semantic mapping.

### 2.3. Logic & Discovery

- **`/dia` (Krisis Judgment)**: Verification and critique (Audit, Steelman, Devil's Advocate, Cold Mirror).
- **`/noe` (Noēsis Recognition)**: Deep recognition for paradigm shifts (Nous, Phro, Meta).
- **`/zet` (Zētēsis Discovery)**: Identifying "correct questions" and anomalies (Anom, Hypo, Eval, Abduction).

## 3. Specialized Modes

- **`/u` (Subjective Dialogue)**: AI subjectivity switch.
  - `/u`: Light opinion.
  - `/u!`: Deep dive with counter-questions.
  - `/u?`: Unconstrained "cast off" dialogue.
- **`/mek` (Mekhanē Method Generation)**: Structural construction.
  - `Functional Beauty`: v7.1 redesign utilizing semantic tables (R1-R6 rules) for high-token efficiency.
  - `visual`: High-fidelity UI/Infographic generation patterns.
  - `manual`: Precise, hallucination-resistant manual/code generation.
